Common questions

Everything you need to know.

Do I need a credit card to start?
No. You can start your 14-day free trial with just an email address. We'll only ask for payment details if you decide to continue.
Can I change plans later?
Yes — you can upgrade or downgrade at any time from your account settings. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle.
Does Competent Management process payroll?
No. We capture accurate, reviewable labor data and export it in payroll-ready format. You run payroll through your existing processor or accountant.
What happens when my trial ends?
You'll be prompted to select a plan. If you don't, your account is paused — your data is never deleted.
Is there a setup fee?
Never. You can be fully operational in under 10 minutes — no implementation fees, no onboarding calls required.
Can I see it before signing up?
Yes — try the live scheduling demo with real sample data, no account needed.
