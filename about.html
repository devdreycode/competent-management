 Our mission

Built for managers who need
numbers they can trust.

Competent Management is a workforce operations platform designed to capture accurate time data — so payroll decisions are based on reality, not guesswork.

What we do

Most payroll problems start
with unclear time records.

Competent Management captures reliable time evidence first, giving managers confidence before payroll is ever run.

We focus on clarity, accountability, and reviewable labor records — without bloated HR systems or unnecessary complexity.

We don't process payroll, issue payments, or file taxes. Our role is to provide accurate, reviewable labor data so payroll can be handled confidently using your existing systems.

  • PIN-based employee kiosk time clock
  • Clock-in, clock-out, and break tracking
  • Time evidence & full audit logs
  • Overtime detection & calculation
  • Monthly payroll prep summaries
  • CSV exports for payroll or accounting
  • Scheduling & coverage gap alerts
